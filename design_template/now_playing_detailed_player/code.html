Welcome to Ốc Oanh. As you approach the stall, you'll immediately be hit by the intoxicating aroma of garlic frying in oil and the smoky scent of charcoal grills. This isn't just a restaurant; it's an institution on Vinh Khanh Street.

Look at the vibrant red chilies adorning the plates. The dish in front of you is the famous "Ốc Hương" - sweet snails stir-fried with salted egg yolk. Notice the rich, creamy texture of the sauce...

The atmosphere here is quintessentially Saigon. Plastic chairs, bustling crowds, and the constant clinking of glasses. It's loud, it's messy, and it's absolutely delicious.
